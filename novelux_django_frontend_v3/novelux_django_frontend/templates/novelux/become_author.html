Fill Details

Complete your personal profile and story details in accordance with our requirements.

Upload

Submit the complete version of your manuscript under the "New Stories" section.

Formatting is Key: Organize your manuscript according to the specified sample format. Chapter Titles must start with "Chapter", use Arabic numerals, and be in the correct sequential order.

Review

Your manuscript will undergo a review by our editorial department.

Listing

Only upon successful completion of the review will your story be listed on Novelux — making it searchable and readable for millions of readers.

04

Assessment

We assess story performance following a listing period.

Contracting

Stories that meet our contracting criteria will receive an invitation offering both non-exclusive and exclusive contract options.
