1. Authorship & Originality

By joining the Novelux author program, you confirm that all manuscripts you submit are your original work, written entirely by you or under a valid co-authorship arrangement. You warrant that your work does not infringe any copyright, trademark, or other intellectual property rights of any third party. Plagiarism of any kind will result in immediate account termination and potential legal action.

2. Content Standards

All content submitted to Novelux must comply with our Content Guidelines. Prohibited content includes but is not limited to: sexual content involving minors, targeted hate speech, content designed to incite violence, and doxxing of real individuals. Violations may result in content removal, account suspension, or permanent termination without compensation.

3. Royalties & Payments

Authors receive 50% of net advertising revenue generated by their stories (the "Royalty Share" model), unless a different arrangement is specified in a separately signed contract. Payments are processed weekly. You are solely responsible for reporting and paying applicable taxes on all earnings. Novelux reserves the right to withhold payments if fraudulent activity or policy violations are suspected.

4. Intellectual Property Licence

You retain full ownership of your original works. By submitting content to Novelux, you grant us a non-exclusive, royalty-free, worldwide, sublicensable licence to host, reproduce, distribute, display, and promote your content on and through the Novelux platform for the duration of your author agreement. This licence ends when your content is removed from the platform, unless otherwise agreed in a separate contract.

5. Manuscript Formatting

All submitted manuscripts must follow the formatting guidelines provided in the Author Portal. Chapter titles must begin with the word "Chapter" followed by an Arabic numeral in correct sequential order. Novelux reserves the right to reject manuscripts that do not meet formatting standards without prejudice.

6. Exclusivity

Standard author accounts are non-exclusive. You may publish the same story on other platforms unless you sign an exclusive contract with Novelux. If you hold an exclusive contract, publishing the same chapters elsewhere constitutes a breach and may result in clawback of payments made.

7. Account Conduct

You agree not to engage in fraudulent read inflation, spam, manipulation of platform ranking systems, or any other activity designed to unfairly benefit your account. Novelux uses automated and manual systems to detect abuse. Confirmed abuse results in immediate suspension and forfeiture of any pending payments.

8. Termination

Either party may terminate the author relationship at any time. Upon termination, your access to the author dashboard is revoked. Published content subject to an active contract will remain on the platform for the contract duration. Pending earnings at the time of termination will be paid in accordance with the standard withdrawal schedule.

9. Amendments

Novelux may update these Author Terms from time to time. You will be notified of material changes via email at least 14 days before they take effect. Continued use of your author account after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the updated terms.
