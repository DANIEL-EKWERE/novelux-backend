You can download novels for offline reading directly from the novel's detail page.

1

Open the novel you want to download.

2

Tap the Download icon (arrow pointing down) in the top-right corner of the novel page.

3

Select the chapters you want — or tap Download All to get everything at once.

4

Once downloaded, the novel appears in your Library → Downloaded tab, accessible without an internet connection.

Download screen screenshot

The download icon appears in the top-right of every novel page.

💡 Downloads are tied to your account. If you log out, downloaded novels may not be accessible until you log in again.

Garbled or scrambled characters are typically caused by a text encoding mismatch between the author's submission file and the app's reader.

Common causes:

!

The chapter was uploaded using a non-UTF-8 encoding (e.g. GBK or ANSI).

!

The chapter contains special characters or symbols not supported by the current font.

How to fix: Use the underline error reporting feature — long-press on the garbled text and select "Report Error." Our editorial team will fix it within 48 hours.

⚠️ If the entire novel appears garbled, try closing and reopening the app, or clearing the app cache in your phone settings.

Sharing a novel generates a link that takes your friend directly to the novel's page on Novelux.

2

A novel may be tagged as "Completed" on Novelux while the original author is still publishing chapters on another platform. This happens because:

!

The version uploaded to Novelux was a completed manuscript at time of submission, but the author continues a new arc or sequel elsewhere.

!

The author has not yet submitted the latest chapters to Novelux for review.

If you believe this is an error, tap "Report Issue" on the novel page and our team will investigate within 72 hours.
